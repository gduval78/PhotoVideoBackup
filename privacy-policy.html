Privacy Policy for PhotoVideoBackup

Last updated: April 2, 2026

PhotoVideoBackup does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information.

All files processed by the app (photos, videos) are transferred directly from your device to an external storage drive connected via USB-C. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any network communication.
